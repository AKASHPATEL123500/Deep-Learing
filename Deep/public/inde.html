ARCHITECTURE

Transformer Kya Hai?

2017 mein Google ne "Attention Is All You Need" paper diya — aur AI revolution aa gayi. BERT, GPT, ChatGPT sab transformer pe based hain.

Problem Kya Thi Pehle?

❌ RNN/LSTM Problem

Sequential processing — ek word ke baad dusra. Long sentences mein pehla word bhool jaata tha. Slow training.

✅ Transformer Solution

Parallel processing — sab words ek saath dekho. Long-range dependencies handle karo. Fast training on GPU!

Transformer Architecture — Block by Block

STEP 1 — INPUT EMBEDDING + POSITIONAL ENCODING
Words ko numbers mein convert karo (embedding). Phir position ka information add karo — "ye word sentence mein 3rd position pe hai". Transformer ko sequence ka order nahi pata hota, isliye manually add karte hain.
↓
STEP 2 — MULTI-HEAD SELF-ATTENTION
Har word dusre words pe "attend" karta hai — kitna related hoon main unse? "Bank" word ka matlab context pe depend karta hai — "river bank" vs "bank account". Multiple heads = multiple aspects pe focus simultaneously.
Query (Q)
Kya dhundh raha?
×
Key (K)
Mere paas kya?
→
Attention
Score
×
Value (V)
Actual info
↓
STEP 3 — ADD & NORM (RESIDUAL CONNECTION)
Original input + attention output ko add karo. Phir normalize karo. Ye "shortcut" deep networks mein gradients ko flow karne deta hai — ResNet ka idea!
↓
STEP 4 — FEED FORWARD NETWORK
Simple 2-layer neural network — attention ke output ko aur process karo. Ye "thinking" karta hai — attention ne jo context diya use transform karta hai. Har position independently process hota hai.
↓
STEP 5 — REPEAT N TIMES (LAYERS)
Ye poora block N baar stack hota hai. GPT-3 mein 96 layers hain! Deeper = more complex patterns learn kar sakta hai.

LLM

LLM Kaise Sochta Hai?

LLM = Large Language Model. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini sab LLM hain. Ye simply "next word predict" karte hain — lekin itne accurate ki conversation jaisa lagta hai!

Tokenization — Pehla Step

Text ko tokens mein todha jaata hai. Token = word ya word ka part.

Input: "Deep Learning bahut powerful hai"

Deep Learn ing bah ut powerful hai

7 tokens. "Learning" → "Learn" + "ing" (subword tokenization)

LLM Training — Ek Simple Example

Training Data (Internet ka sab text):

"The cat sat on the ___"

Model predict karta hai: "mat" (95%), "floor" (3%), "dog" (1%)...

Ye next-word prediction billions baar karne ke baad model language samajh leta hai.

ChatGPT ko "Helpful" Kaise Banaya? — RLHF

STEP 1
Pre-training

Internet ka sab text pe train — Wikipedia, books, code, articles. Trillions of tokens. Sirf "next word predict" karna seekha.

STEP 2
Supervised Fine-tuning (SFT)

Human trainers ne examples likhe — "Question X ka achha answer Y hota hai". Model ne ye examples se seekha.

STEP 3
Reward Model Training

Humans ne multiple answers rank kiye — "A better hai B se". Ek reward model train kiya jo judge kare kaunsa answer achha hai.

STEP 4
RLHF — Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback

Reward model ki feedback se LLM ke weights update kiye — helpful, harmless, honest responses ke liye optimize kiya. Ye Claude ka bhi secret hai! 😄

Attention Mechanism

Word pe click karo — dekho model kaunse words pe zyada "attend" karta hai (highlighted = high attention).

SENTENCE 1 — Click any word:

The bank was near the river

SENTENCE 2 — Click any word:

I deposited money at the bank

Samajh aaya? 🎯

Sentence 1 mein "bank" → "river" pe high attention = nadi ka kinara.
Sentence 2 mein "bank" → "deposited", "money" pe high attention = bank account.

Same word, alag context, alag meaning — Transformer ye automatically samajhta hai!

Multi-Head Attention = Multiple Perspectives

Head 1

Syntax pe focus — subject-verb agreement

Head 2

Coreference — "he" = kaun?

Head 3

Semantic meaning — word relationships
